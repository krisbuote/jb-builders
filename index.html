Home Renovation

Undertaking a renovation is often a stressful time for homeowners. Who do you hire? How do you know what needs to be done? It's an overwhelming process, but don't worry, we are here to help. Whether it's your kitchen, bathroom, basement, or full house, we have you covered. We always keep a clean and organized work space, paying attention to the personal dynamic involved in home renovations.

New Builds

We provide in-house carpentry services, specializing in framing and finishing. We build everything from homes, garages, decks, to commercial spaces and furniture. Jared Buote, the owner of JB Builders, is a carpenter by trade. You will see him on every project, taking pride in his craft.

Project Management

We bring together only the finest individuals who excel at their trade. JB Builders works on behalf of you, the client, holding your vision and best interest as top priority. As a small builder, we take on one project at a time. We keep a small circle of reliable industry professionals who excel at their trade. This ensures that your needs come first and that the project is done right and within budget.

Jared Buote

Owner

Hey, I'm Jared, the owner of JB Builders. I formed JB Builders in 2016. Since then we've been serving Calgary and Central Alberta. We specialize in new builds, renovations, and project management.

At JB Builders, my primary focus is working with good, trustworthy people. I know from experience that construction projects are successful only when relationships are prioritized. I build with like-minded individuals to keep an open, communicative environment for both clients and contractors alike. This offers a space to float new ideas, while keeping my clients 'in the know'. I believe the size of my company makes this possible. I keep a small, well informed circle of honest trade professionals who are committed to getting it done right, one project at a time. This accessible, down to earth approach has made JB Builders easy to work with, and successful in Calgary and greater area. Give me a call today, I'm open to discuss any project. Let's collaborate and build your vision.

- JB
