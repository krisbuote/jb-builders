Calgary and Central Alberta

Home Renovation

Undertaking a renovation is often a stressful time for homeowners. Who do you hire? How do you know what needs to be done? It's an overwhelming process, but don't worry, we are here to help. Whether it's your kitchen, bathroom, basement, or full house, we have you covered. We always keep a clean and organized work space, paying attention to the personal dynamic involved in home renovations.

New Builds

We provide in-house carpentry services, specializing in framing and finishing. We build everything from homes, garages, decks, to commercial spaces and furniture. Jared Buote, the owner of JB Builders, is a carpenter by trade. You will see him on every project, taking pride in his craft.

Project Management

We bring together only the firest individuals who excel at their trade. JB Builders works on behalf of you, the client, holding your vision and best interest as top priority. As a small builder, we take on one project at a time. We keep a small circle of reliable industry professionals who excel at their trade. This ensures that your needs come first, and the project is done right and within budget.
