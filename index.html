Services

Calgary and Central Alberta

Home Renovation

Renovations can be a stressful time for homeowners. Where do you start? Who do you hire? What's even possible? It can be an overwhelming process, but don't worry, we are here to help. Whether it's a kitchen reno, bathroom, basement, or full house, we have you covered. We keep a clean and organized work space, paying close attention to the personal dynamic involved in home renovations.

Custom Builder

We specialize in residential building with in-house carpentry services. This includes full homes, framing, finishing, built-ins, garages, decks, pergolas, and some furniture. Jared Buote, the owner of JB Builders, is a carpenter by trade. You will see him on every project, taking pride in his craft.

Project Management

Construction is all about planning and executing. There is a lot to consider before you actually build. Things like design, blueprints, permits, hiring contractors, picking products, finishings, fixtures, etc. These things need to be decided and planned in order to build effectively. This is where we come in. JB Builders works with professionals to plan and prepare you project, while we manage the entire process. As part of our service, we provide regular schedule and budget updates to keep you informed along the way.
